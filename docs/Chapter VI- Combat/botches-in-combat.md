## Botches in Combat

These combat rules tell players who wins a fight and indicate how much damage each side suffers, but they alone cannot simulate all the life-threatening, anxiety-provoking, and even humorous things that can happen on a battlefield. Botches add unpredictable possibilities into the mix.

In combat (as in any other stressful situation), when a stress die comes up 0, botch dice are rolled. As a general rule, assume that multiple botches are that many times as bad as a single botch. In general, a one-die botch (when one zero comes up on the botch dice) is not harmful, only embarrassing or inconvenient, while a four-die botch could very well be highly injurious or perhaps fatal.
